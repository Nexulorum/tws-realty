About TWS Realty

Helping Families Find a Place to Call Home

TWS Realty was created to help individuals and families navigate the rental process with greater clarity, personal attention, and dependable guidance.

Whether someone is searching for a long-term rental, short-term housing, or Section 8 opportunities, the goal is to make the process feel less overwhelming and easier to understand.

Trish Smith founded TWS Realty with a commitment to responsive communication, honest guidance, and helping people move forward with confidence as they search for their next home.
